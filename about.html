TAKE A LOOK AT OUR HISTORY


Wanderlust Travel Agency is one of the leading travel agencies in the Philippines. The head office is located at Quezon City. The company also has different branches all over the Philippines. We started operating on October 23, 2018 as a privately held company, not affiliated with the Philippines Government. After a short two years in business, and seeing success, incorporated with the Philippines Security and Exchange Commission, and has since expanded its operations to include all 7,107 Philippine Islands.

Since our humble beginnings on 2018 we have delivered a satisfactory service to hundreds of tourist all over the Philippines.

The founders of Wanderlust Travel Agency is composed of a group of friends who loves traveling and they are Patricia Anne Octavo, Chrizl Diana Corpuz, Khate Julia Carantes and Ma. Cassandra Gwyneth Bontogon. They are dedicated travel professionals providing quality customer experiences for travel and tourism in the Philippines.

With your safety and enjoyment foremost in our minds, we have maintained solid and long-term partnership with hotels and resorts all over the Philippines.

We at Wanderlust Travel Agency are proud of our reputation for excellence. Bringing you extraordinary experiences, what are you waiting for?
